WHO IS SONICFOX?

Dominique McLean, better known by SonicFox, is an American professional esports player of several fighting games. McLean is recognized for their versatility to pick up a new game or character and master it for professional play relatively quickly.

McLean has topped many tournaments, including five Evolution Championship Series (EVO) events for four different game franchises. They are the highest paid fighting game esports player in the world as of August 4, 2019, with over $600,000 in earnings.
