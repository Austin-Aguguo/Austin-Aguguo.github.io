EPIC MOMENTS IN E-SPORTS

E-SPORTS PLAYER PARRIES 30-HIT COMBO!

This was one of the most iconic moments in e-sports history! Imagining being skillful enough to parry every signle hit of a 30-hit combo while under the pressure of being watched by hundreds of people! It would be nice to have Nigerian gamers step up and provide the world with epic highlights like this.


SONICFOX HUMBLES A TRASH-TALKER

Imagine beating the person who has been cyber-bullying you for months, in your favourite videogame. E-sports is not just a competitive and fun avenue, but it is also a medium, in the midst of this toxic internet cyberspace, to give children the confidence to publicly prove themselves in areas where they have skills, in the face of their haters.
